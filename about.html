WHY THE OTTOMAN ?

The Ottoman Carpets and Home Living is based in Türkiye. We started our Company since 2016. Our Products All from Türkiye and Made in Türkiye from Rugs handmade and machine carpets, Mosaic Lamp, Bedding sets, Textiles, Prayers mats, Dinner set, Virgin Olive oil. The Ottoman represents timeless luxury rooted in authentic Turkish heritage. Each piece is originally crafted and sourced from Turkey, reflecting refined artistry, rich textures, and elegant details inspired by the Ottoman era. Designed for those who value exclusivity, culture, and understated luxury.
